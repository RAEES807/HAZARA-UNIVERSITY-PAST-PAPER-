Hazara University

LLB (Five Years) Part-1st Annual Examination, 2021

Paper: Introduction to Logic & Reasoning and Introduction to Psychology

Time Allowed: 3 hrs

Marks: 100

Note: Attempt any five questions in all. Two questions from Part A and two questions from Part B are compulsory to attempt. All questions carry equal marks.

PART-A (Introduction to Psychology) (50)
  1. What are the stages of memory? Also give theory of forgetting.
  2. Define psychology? What are the areas of psychology that are very functional in Pakistan?
  3. What are the types of thinking? Explain the tools of thinking?
  4. Give the factors of perception in detail?
  5. Define sensation. Discuss the function of different sensation?

PART-B (Logic and Reasoning) (50)
  1. Discuss the following in detail:
    1. Language and its basic function
    2. Definition and its types
  2. Discuss the four forms (standard forms) of categorical proposition with example.
  3. Explain the different forms of fallacies with examples.
  4. What is categorical syllogism? Also explain the terms of a categorical syllogism.
  5. How categorical propositions oppose each other? Explain with the help of traditional square proposition?
